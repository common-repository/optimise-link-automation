== Description ==
The Optimise Linkables plugin makes managing Affiliate links on your Wordpress site simple. It automatically converts all outbound website links in your articles into Optimise Affiliate Tracking links where the link belongs to an Optimise Advertiser so you can earn commission on sales you refer to an Advertiser.

As our Affiliate, this allows you to monetize your content by easily converting your linkable assets with tracking links automatically.

Optimise has over 3000 brands available in 20 countries, across a wide range of sectors, from  fashion, travel and retail to food and entertainment.

= Benefits =

* Automatically creates deeplinks for each product mentioned in your content.
* Increased organic search traffic 
* Increased referral traffic
* Increased brand exposure and affinity

= Getting started =

1. Add the Affiliate specific script on all your content pages  (steps shown on the next slide)
2. Join the Advertiser Campaigns applicable to your content in [Dashboard](https://dashboard.optimisemedia.com/ "Optimise Dashboard")
3. Create the relevant external links for your content as usual 
4. The plugin automatically convert links to Affiliate Links using Optimise Link Automation
5. Earn commission for sales you generate

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can anyone use the plugin? =

You must be an Optimise Affiliate. Yon can sign up here: https://www.optimisemedia.com/apply

For existing Publishers login here: https://dashboard.optimisemedia.com/

= Where will I find my AID? =

* Login to your account at https://dashboard.optimisemedia.com/
* Click on the User icon in the top right-hand corner
* Your AID is listed as ID at the bottom of this 

= Where can I find out more? =

Check out our Link Automation documentation here: https://knowledge.optimisemedia.com/docs/publishertools#link-automation

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin
2. Go to Settings > Optimise Link Automation
3. Configure your Optimise AID (Affiliate ID/Publisher ID)
